Training with your cycle

Follicular (Days 1–13): Oestrogen rising — strength, motivation and recovery are building. Great time to push heavier and try new movements.

Ovulation (Day 14): Peak strength and drive. Your best sessions often fall here.

Luteal (Days 15–28): Progesterone rises — you may feel heavier and fatigue faster. Completely normal. Prioritise showing up over hitting PRs.

Menstrual (Days 1–5): Energy often lower. Focus on movement quality. The fact you're there is the win.
